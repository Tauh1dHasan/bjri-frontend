Our History

Advancing Jute Research Since 1970

The Bangladesh Jute Research Institute has been at the forefront of jute research and development, contributing to sustainable agricultural practices and enhancing the quality of jute production for over five decades.
1970

Establishment of the Institute

The Bangladesh Jute Research Institute was established to promote research and development in jute cultivation and processing.
1985

Introduction of New Varieties

The institute introduced high-yielding and disease-resistant jute varieties to enhance productivity.
2000

Launch of Sustainable Practices

The institute began promoting sustainable farming practices to improve soil health and reduce environmental impact.
2015

Global Collaborations

The institute established partnerships with international research organizations to enhance jute research and development.
